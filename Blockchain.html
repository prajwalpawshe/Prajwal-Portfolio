Counterfeit Drugs Detection Using Blockchain

A Solution to Ensure Drug Authenticity and Patient Safety

Project Overview

This project aims to develop a blockchain-based solution to combat counterfeit drugs in the pharmaceutical supply chain. By leveraging blockchain’s transparency and immutability, we can track drugs from manufacturers to end-users, ensuring authenticity and reducing risks associated with counterfeit medications.

Objectives

  • Prevent counterfeit drugs from entering the market.
  • Enhance transparency and trust in the pharmaceutical supply chain.
  • Ensure traceability from manufacturer to consumer.
  • Reduce health risks associated with fake medications.

Benefits of Blockchain in Drug Detection

Implementing blockchain offers several benefits in preventing counterfeit drugs:

  • Transparency: All parties in the supply chain have visibility into the drug’s journey.
  • Immutability: Once recorded, data cannot be altered, preventing tampering.
  • Traceability: Each step in the drug’s lifecycle is recorded and accessible for verification.
  • Accountability: Increases trust and accountability among manufacturers, distributors, and retailers.

Technology Stack

  • Blockchain Platform: Ethereum, Hyperledger
  • Smart Contracts: For secure, automated transactions and tracking.
  • Front-end: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Back-end: Node.js, Solidity (for smart contracts)
  • Database: IPFS (InterPlanetary File System) for secure, decentralized data storage
